About PinkScan

PinkScan is a pioneering AI-powered platform designed to transform breast cancer detection and prevention. Leveraging advanced machine learning and a commitment to accessibility, we empower individuals and healthcare professionals worldwide with tools for early intervention, precise diagnostics, and personalized health strategies.

Our Mission

At PinkScan, our mission is to harness technology to save lives by making breast cancer detection faster, more accurate, and universally accessible. We strive to reduce the global burden of breast cancer through innovation, education, and collaboration. Our core objectives include:

  • Enabling early detection to improve survival rates and treatment outcomes.
  • Delivering affordable, scalable solutions for underserved communities.
  • Raising awareness about breast health and preventive care.
  • Partnering with medical experts to integrate cutting-edge research into practical tools.
  • Ensuring privacy and security with state-of-the-art data protection.

Our Vision

We envision a future where breast cancer is no longer a silent threat, but a manageable condition detected early and treated effectively. By bridging the gap between advanced technology and everyday users, PinkScan aims to create a global community empowered with knowledge and resources to thrive.

Our Technology

PinkScan is built on a robust Random Forest Classifier, trained on the renowned Breast Cancer Wisconsin (Diagnostic) Dataset, analyzing 30 critical features—such as clump thickness, cell size uniformity, and mitotic activity—to predict tumor malignancy with high precision. Key features of our platform include:

  • Real-time predictive analytics with over 95% accuracy.
  • Interactive, multilingual dashboards for seamless user experience.
  • Comprehensive PDF reports with dietary and lifestyle recommendations.
  • Secure cloud-based infrastructure with end-to-end encryption.
  • Ongoing enhancements driven by the latest advancements in AI and oncology.
